Revelation of Shadows

A 2D Pixel Art RPG with Metroidvania & Roguelike Elements

Developer: Meng Minghao
Project Type: Final Year Project (FYP)

Project Description

Revelation of Shadows is a 2D pixel art RPG that blends Metroidvania, Roguelike, and action-adventure mechanics. The story follows Liana, a warrior seeking to reforge the Holy Artifact of Light to banish the darkness consuming the land. Players will explore an open-ended world, face challenging enemies, and uncover deep lore through non-linear storytelling.
